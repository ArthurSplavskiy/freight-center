How do i ship an engine?

The best way to ship an engine is via LTL Freight. This shipping method allows your engine to occupy only the necessary amount of space needed within an enclosed freight truck.

By consolidating your engine shipment with other shippers’ freight, you to effectively “share the cost” of a full truckload. Because of this, shipping your engine via LTL freight could cost as low as $100+, depending on weight, distance, and other factors.
