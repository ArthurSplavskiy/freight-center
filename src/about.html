Our Mission

To create value for our customers by delivering customized shipping solutions that meet their unique needs and to fulfill shipping demands from simple to complex with expertise, guidance and ingenuity.

Who we are: Relationship-based shipping solutions

In 1998, Matthew Brosious, President & CEO founded FreightCenter and we began reinventing the shipping process. Since then, we’ve helped thousands of businesses and individuals all over the world get their goods from point A to point B faster and smarter.

@@include('html/_delivery-form.htm', {})

It all began by servicing the transactional freight shipping needs for the everyday consumer. We soon discovered that our customer’s shipping needs were changing. Shippers want a partner, someone who understands their unique needs. They want experts in logistics to help remove inefficiencies and handle the process.

Today, FreightCenter is a privately owned, full-service 3PL with a vast portfolio of transportation solutions from rail to truckload to parcel packages. As we move the shipping and logistics industry into the future, we are committed to delivering a streamlined and simplified shipping experience.
